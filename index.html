About Ek Makān

Ek Makān
Inclusive Property Platform

We started Ek Makān because finding a home in India shouldn't depend on your background.

Across India's cities, countless families face silent rejection when searching for a place to live — turned away by landlords or brokers based on who they are, not what kind of tenants or buyers they would be. This isn't just unfair. It is a quiet crisis that affects millions of families every year.

Ek Makān was built to change that. We are India's first property platform built on a single founding principle: every family deserves a home, and every home should be open to every family.

What makes us different

Every listing on Ek Makān comes from an owner or broker who has explicitly agreed to our Inclusion Charter — a commitment that their property is open to all prospective residents without discrimination of any kind. This isn't just a checkbox. It is a verified promise.

We also make the process transparent: verified badges, RERA registration details, contact information, and multiple photos are all available to registered users — so you can make an informed decision before ever picking up the phone.

Our mission

To make the search for a home in India a dignified experience for every family — regardless of background, community, dietary preference, profession, or any other factor unrelated to tenancy.

The team

Ek Makān was founded in 2025 and is headquartered in Mumbai, Maharashtra. Our team includes technologists, real estate professionals, and advocates who believe that access to housing is not a privilege — it is a right.

"A home is not just four walls. It is where a family grows, rests, and belongs. At Ek Makān, we exist to ensure that belonging is never denied."

— Ek Makān Founding Team

Inclusion Charter

📜
Every lister on Ek Makān signs this charter
By posting a property on Ek Makān, owners and brokers formally agree to these principles. Violations result in immediate removal and permanent ban.

The Ek Makān Inclusion Charter

Effective from: January 2025  ·  Version 1.0

1
Open to All Residents

I agree to consider all prospective tenants and buyers on the basis of financial eligibility and tenancy history alone. I will not screen, reject, or discourage any applicant on the basis of their community, background, dietary preferences, profession, marital status, or any factor unrelated to their suitability as a tenant or buyer.

2
Honest Listings

All information I provide — including photos, price, location, area, amenities, and availability — is accurate and up to date. I will not use misleading descriptions or bait-and-switch pricing.

3
Prompt Communication

I commit to responding to inquiries within a reasonable time. If a property is no longer available, I will remove or update the listing promptly.

4
Privacy of Inquirers

I will use contact details shared by prospective tenants or buyers solely for the purpose of this property transaction. I will not share, sell, or misuse this information.

5
Zero Tolerance for Discrimination

I understand that any verified complaint of discriminatory behaviour will result in immediate removal of all my listings and a permanent ban from the Ek Makān platform. Ek Makān reserves the right to report egregious cases to relevant authorities.

6
Reporting Violations

If you experience or witness a violation of this charter, please contact us immediately. Every report is investigated within 48 hours and action is taken swiftly.

Report a violation: Click the 🚩 Report button on any listing, or use this form directly. We investigate every report within 48 hours.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026  ·  HAFAR CONTRATECH AI LLP

Ek Makān ("we", "our", "the platform") is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights in relation to it.

1. Information We Collect

Account information: When you register, we collect your name, email address, phone number, and role (tenant, owner, or broker).

Listing information: Owners and brokers provide property details, photos, and contact information for their listings.

Inquiry data: When you contact a property owner, we store the message and your contact details to facilitate the transaction.

Usage data: We may collect anonymised data on how users interact with the platform to improve our service.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and improve the Ek Makān platform
  • To connect prospective tenants and buyers with property owners
  • To send you relevant notifications about your account or listings
  • To investigate complaints and enforce the Inclusion Charter
  • To comply with applicable Indian laws and regulations

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. Your contact details are shared with property owners only when you submit an inquiry. Property owner contact details are shared with registered users only after they log in.

4. Data Storage

Currently, Ek Makān stores data locally in your browser (localStorage). No data is transmitted to external servers at this stage of the platform. As we scale, data will be stored securely on servers located in India, compliant with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023.

5. Your Rights

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time

6. Cookies

Ek Makān does not currently use tracking cookies. We may use essential session storage to maintain your login state within a session.

7. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or requests, please use the Contact page on this platform. We will respond within 5 working days.

This policy is subject to change as the platform evolves. Material changes will be communicated to registered users by email or platform notification.
